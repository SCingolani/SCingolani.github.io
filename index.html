About me

Your scientists were so preoccupied with whether or not they could, they didn't stop to think if they should.

I always face questions of the like of: “Why are you doing that?” “Why don't you just buy it?”. The answer is that I enjoy the process; the process of researching, the process of learning, the process of designing, and the process of building something myself, rather than just buying an off-the-shelf solution. As such I find myself always on the lookout for new projects to teach myself something new, mostly within the realms of software development, electronics, and occasionally music. This philosophy permeates my life so don't be surprised if one day you find me fermenting ginger to make beer or researching how to make cheese.
